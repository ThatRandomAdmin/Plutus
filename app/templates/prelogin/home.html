Plutus

This application will help to turn your financial troubles into a thing of the past!

Purpose

I built Plutus to demonstrate my ability to design and develop a simple, functional python web app as a part of my degree apprenticeship.

Functionality

The app allows users to upload bank statements and reconcile them with their own self-entered transactions. It also has multiple tiers of accounts, to allow for actions to be blocked based on the users permission level.

Warning

This application is a demonstration of my skills and is not intended for use with real data. I cannot stress this enough, please do not upload any real data. I am not responsible for any data loss or issues that may arise from using this application.
